Elopements and small ceremonies

Elopement shooting, more intimate

An elopement is not a smaller version of a wedding, It is more dependent on location, weather, two people's status and route planning, Fiona will prioritize explaining these variables clearly to avoid customers only seeing beautiful photos but not knowing how to implement them

Initiate an elopement photography consultation

Service process simplified version

  1. Submit an inquiry stating date, location and type of shoot
  2. Communicate needs, budget, timelines, and site conditions
  3. Confirm the plan, quotation, contract and deposit rules
  4. Prepare a timeline and focus list before shooting
  5. Official shooting, subsequent film selection, editing and delivery

You can prepare before consulting

You don’t need to figure out all the details at once, Just tell Fiona the date, location, number of people, type of ceremony and the look and feel you like, and you can start to judge the schedule and plan
