Guide to filming locations in the South of France

Suitable for 2-person proposal, friend witness, light decoration upgrade and couple photo after proposal, The following locations are divided into public accessible locations and paid venues that require advance reservation; actual implementation will be confirmed based on weather, light, crowd flow, traffic, and venue rules

Consultation date and location View point reference

Suitable for whom

Ideal for those who want a lightweight proposal in Nice, Monaco or the Côte d’Azur: it can be a quiet moment for two people, or you can include a bouquet, a witness from friends, a short video or a post-proposal photo

How to determine the location

I will confirm the route based on your date, number of people, city range, proposal style, whether you need bouquets or friends to witness, combined with the light, flow of people and traffic on the day

How to execute it on the day

Proposal photography is different from ordinary appointment photography, The photographer will arrive in advance to confirm the camera location and movement route, focusing on ensuring that the moment of proposal is recorded naturally and completely

Location confirmation logic

First confirm whether the day will go well

The points below are not fixed templates, The final recommendation will be confirmed based on the date, number of people, itinerary, budget, whether flowers or friends are needed to witness, as well as the weather and crowds on the day

01

Screen background

Bays, ancient castles, colorful towns, mountain and sea vistas, city landmarks or hotel terraces will determine the sense of destination and ceremony in the photos

02

traffic routes

Driving distance from Nice, train frequency, walking distance, uphill and downhill slopes, parking and transfer times will all affect whether the day is suitable for execution

03

Privacy is stable

Public spots cannot guarantee that no one will be watching; if you want more stability and privacy, you can consider restaurants, hotel terraces or garden venues

Public spots and paid venues

Reference points for marriage proposal in the South of France

The following points are divided into public accessible points and paid venues that require advance reservation, All public spots need to be reconfirmed based on the day's light, flow of people, weather and traffic; if you choose a restaurant, hotel terrace, garden ticket or authorized area, the venue fee will be charged separately

Publicly accessible / strong sense of destination

Seaside castle line

The chateau, bay, red rocks and gardens are all in the same area, perfect for proposals that want a "Southern French destination feel". When you don't want to rearrange, the background itself is enough.

Suitable

  • I hope there are clear memories of the South of France in the photos
  • I want to capture the atmosphere of the seaside, castle and garden in the same area

Notice

  • The rocky areas are not suitable for stiletto heels and walking shoes are recommended
  • There will be tourists in summer and weekends, so adjustments are needed when the wind is strong or the sea conditions are bad

Publicly accessible/Colorful Town

menton bay line

Menton's colorful old town, bay, port and mountain background are combined together, the South of France feels more colorful and slower here than in Nice and Monaco

Suitable

  • I am willing to take the train from Nice and combine the proposal with a walk in the small town
  • I like soft, bright images with less crowded backgrounds

Notice

  • After arriving at Menton Railway Station, you still need to walk to the appropriate point
  • Rock or step areas are not suitable for high heels and the midday light in summer is hard

Nice city / most convenient

Nice city sightseeing line

Located next to the Old Town of Nice, it is one of the most accessible city viewing spots, You can see the bay, the old town, the Promenade des Anglais and the port of Limpia

Suitable

  • Time is tight and I just want to complete the proposal and shoot in Nice
  • I hope that after the proposal, I can go directly back to the old town or the beach to continue shooting

Notice

  • This is a popular viewing spot and privacy cannot be guaranteed
  • It will be hot when walking up the mountain in summer, and the elevator may not always be open

Near Nice / Open vistas

Nice mountain and sea view line

Not far from downtown Nice, it is suitable for customers who want a broad background of mountains and sea, The picture will be more spacious than the urban spots, and more layered than the pure seaside

Suitable

  • I want a background with a balanced proportion of mountains, sea and city
  • Prefer natural styles, distant compositions and wider views

Notice

  • Tourists and tour buses may be present during peak seasons or on weekends
  • The sea and sky may appear white when there is strong light, and it is not suitable for complex arrangements when the wind is strong

Quiet vista/sense of travel

mountain bay line

The focus of the picture is not a certain building, but the bay, port, mountain and city levels overlooked from a high place, Overall it's generally quieter than popular viewing spots in the city

Suitable

  • I like nature, a sense of travel, and distant proposals
  • I want a relatively quiet picture with more obvious layers of mountains and seas

Notice

  • It is recommended to take a taxi or drive yourself, as walking alone will be tiring
  • It is not a strong landmark background, and the recognition of the South of France mainly comes from the bay and mountains

Coastal walks/nature documentaries

lighthouse shoreline

The coastal trail runs along rocks, cliffs and the sea, and the view in the direction of the lighthouse is unique. It’s perfect for a “natural proposal on a walk” approach.

Suitable

  • I like the documentary feel, walking style and natural proposals
  • I am willing to spend a little more transportation time in exchange for a more unique coastline picture

Notice

  • The trail is not suitable for high heels or overly formal dresses
  • Parts of the trail may be closed due to strong winds, rough waves or bad weather

Clean sea view/low-key nature

Coastal Trail

The water, rocks and coastal walks provide a clean backdrop for a more low-key, natural proposal, The coastline here is quieter than in urban areas

Suitable

  • No obvious layout is required, just clean sea views and natural records
  • Want to combine it with an itinerary to Monaco, or arrange a small celebration after proposing

Notice

  • There is less content nearby for extended shots
  • The background does not necessarily have a strong identification of southern French landmarks, and rocky areas are not suitable for high heels

Sense of city / sense of landmark

Monaco landmarks frame line

Monaco is suitable for clients who want the image to have a more urban and landmark feel, The visual focus is on ports, city buildings, mountains and more recognizable compositions

Suitable

  • Prefer photos that are more urban, trendy, and have a sense of travel destinations
  • I want to combine my proposal with a one-day trip to Monaco

Notice

  • The flow of people in public areas cannot be controlled and privacy cannot be guaranteed
  • There are many ups and downs and elevators in Monaco, so parking, construction or security need to be judged in advance

Paid/reservation required

Eze Hotel Terrace

The Golden Goat Hotel and the Chateau d'Ezza Hotel are both located in the village of Eze and are suitable for clients who wish to upgrade their proposal to a more private and formal experience

Suitable

  • Hope it can be combined with a sea view terrace, dining experience or hotel services
  • Want a quieter, more dignified, more formal scene than a public spot

Notice

  • Availability, prices and shooting rules must be confirmed with the hotel in advance
  • The budget will be significantly higher than a public spot, and the outdoor terrace will still be affected by the weather

Booking process

After confirmation, refine the daily moving line

At the heart of every proposal is pace and confidentiality, After confirming the date and direction, I will sort out the proposal location, arrival route and on-site rhythm based on your travel arrangements, light, people flow and traffic

Booking and confirmation process

  1. Confirm date, number of people, city range and proposal style
  2. Choose a shooting package based on your budget; if it involves venue, makeup and hair, video or floral arrangement upgrades, confirm the quote separately
  3. The schedule is subject to the deposit confirmation, and the cancellation and rescheduling rules are subject to the final confirmation information
  4. Confirm the proposal location, arrival route and on-site rhythm based on weather, light, crowd flow and itinerary
  5. On the day, the photographer arrived in advance to confirm the location, and completed the proposal and subsequent shooting according to the on-site conditions
  6. Package finishing drawings are usually delivered within 5 working days, expedited, video and additional delivery requirements can be confirmed separately
