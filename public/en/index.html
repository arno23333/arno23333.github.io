Hello, I’m Fiona Tang

South French photographer based in Nice

I am based in Nice and primarily photograph personal trips, couples, proposals and small celebrations in Nice and nearby Côte d'Azur locations; Provence or other cities in France can be confirmed individually based on dates, transportation and shooting schedule
