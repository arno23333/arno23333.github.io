Frequently asked questions

Here are the questions clients usually ask before booking, including availability, payment, delivery, weather, travel areas and what Fiona needs to know in advance, you can read this first, then send your date, location and ideas

Bookings & Prices

Can I make a last-minute appointment?

Yes, if Fiona still has availability, a session can sometimes be arranged one or two days in advance and there is no extra fee just for asking at the last minute, for popular dates, sunset sessions, proposals or multi-location shoots, confirming earlier makes it easier to secure the best timing

How to arrange reservation and payment?

After the session is confirmed, 30% of the total price is usually paid as a booking fee to reserve the date and begin preparation, the final payment timing will be stated with the confirmed plan, cancellation, rescheduling and booking-fee terms will also be written clearly in the official confirmation

Can we shoot outside Nice?

Yes, Fiona is based in Nice and usually works in Nice and nearby Côte d'Azur locations, Provence, Saint-Tropez or other French cities can be confirmed separately depending on date, travel and schedule

The day of shooting

What should we do if we don’t know how to face the camera?

That is completely fine, Fiona will guide movement, position, interaction and rhythm according to how you feel, if you prefer candid images, the session can stay looser and more documentary, the goal is for the photos to feel like you, not for you to pose the whole time

Is it possible to add time?

If there is room in the schedule later that day, extra time can be discussed on site, any overtime fee will be explained before confirmation, there will be no unexplained charge added after the session

What should I do if it rains or the weather is bad?

Cloudy weather is not always a problem, soft light can be very flattering for photos, if weather seriously affects the session, such as heavy rain, strong wind or unsafe conditions, we can discuss rescheduling, changing the time or adjusting the location

Delivery and follow-up

When can I get the photos?

Most personal, couple, travel, proposal or small sessions are delivered within 2-4 weeks, the exact timing depends on the session scope, number of final images and current schedule, weddings or more complex projects may take longer and will be confirmed before booking

Are RAW files included?

RAW files are not included by default, you receive selected, color-graded and lightly retouched high-resolution JPG files, this keeps the final images consistent with Fiona’s style and avoids confusion from unfinished working files

Do you provide albums, professional printing or more finished photos?

By default, delivery includes selected, color-graded and lightly retouched high-resolution digital images, physical albums, professional prints and additional refined edits are separate add-ons, if you need printed products, Fiona will first confirm the supplier, size, paper, production timeline, shipping method and final quote

Venue and video suggestions

Can you advise on image-friendly timing for our wedding day?

Fiona can suggest locations, light, routes and timing from a photography perspective, and point out which moments are worth reserving photo time for, full wedding planning, vendor management and contract execution would need separate confirmation, her support focuses on making the day easier to photograph and document

Can you suggest venues, makeup or styling resources from a photography perspective?

Fiona can share leads related to location, light, route, timing and visual atmosphere, and can point out choices that may affect the images, full wedding planning, vendor management, price negotiation and contract execution need separate confirmation, specific resources depend on the current project, schedule and location

What do customers need to prepare in advance?

You do not need to have everything prepared from the beginning, start with the approximate date, city or location, session type and the feeling you want the photos to carry, other details can be sorted out after the first consultation
