Frequently Asked Questions Before Consultation

Frequently Asked Questions, please clarify first

Here is a list of the most frequently asked questions before a consultation, including how to make an appointment, how to pay, how to deliver photos, and what circumstances are appropriate to tell Fiona in advance, You can take a look at it first and then come in with a date, location and ideas

Bookings & Prices

Can I make a last-minute appointment?

Can, As long as Fiona has a schedule on the day, you can also make an appointment one or two days in advance, and there will be no additional charges for last-minute consultations, Just for popular dates, sunset periods, proposals, or shoots that require multiple locations, the earlier you confirm, the easier it is to arrange the ideal time

How to arrange reservation and payment?

After confirming the shooting, you usually need to pay 30% of the total price as a reservation fee to reserve the date and start early communication, The final payment time will be stated when confirming the plan, Regarding cancellation, rescheduling and whether the reservation fee will be refunded, it will be clearly stated in the official confirmation information to avoid inconsistent understanding between the two parties

Is it possible to film outside Nice?

Can, Fiona is based in Nice and regular shooting areas include Nice and nearby Côte d'Azur locations; Provence, Saint-Tropez or other French cities can be confirmed individually based on dates, transportation and shooting schedule

The day of shooting

What should we do if we don’t know how to face the camera?

It doesn't matter at all, Fiona will provide guidance on movement, position, interaction and rhythm based on your status, If you prefer natural captures, you can also make the shooting more relaxed, The focus is to make the picture look like you, rather than forcing you to pose all the time

Is it possible to add time?

If there is still room in the schedule later in the day, we can temporarily communicate and add time, Extra time charges will be explained before confirmation, and there will be no sudden increase in unexplained charges after the shoot

What should I do if it rains or the weather is bad?

Generally cloudy days are not necessarily a bad thing, the soft light is actually very suitable for taking pictures, If the weather seriously affects the shooting, such as heavy rain, strong winds or it is obviously unsafe, we can negotiate together to reschedule, change the time or adjust the location

Delivery and follow-up

When can I get the photos?

Full delivery is typically 2-4 weeks for most individuals, couples, travel, proposals or small shoots, The specific time will be confirmed based on the shooting content, number of photos and current schedule, Weddings or more complex projects may take longer and will be stated prior to booking

Will the original film be delivered?

The original film is not delivered by default, What you receive is a high-definition JPG film that has been screened, color graded and basic processed, This ensures that the final photo maintains Fiona’s aesthetic and integrity, and avoids misunderstandings caused by handing unfinished files to clients

Do you provide albums, professional printing or more finished photos?

By default, high-definition electronic films are delivered after screening, color grading and basic processing, Physical photo albums, professional printing and more refined photos are subsequent add-on purchases or separate services and are not currently committed as a default package, If you need physical products, Fiona will first confirm the supplier, size, paper, production cycle, mailing method and final quotation before deciding whether to access it

Venue and video suggestions

Can you provide advice on image angles for your wedding day?

It can provide location, light, route and time suggestions from the shooting perspective, and also remind you which aspects are worth reserving photo time, Full wedding planning, supplier management and contract execution are subject to confirmation; Fiona's support focuses on making the day arrangement suitable for filming and video recording

Can you provide clues about location, makeup or layout resources from a shooting perspective?

You can share resource clues related to location, light, route, timing and picture atmosphere from an image perspective, and also be reminded of which choices may affect the shooting, Complete wedding planning, supplier management, price negotiation and contract execution need to be confirmed separately; specific resources are subject to the confirmation results of the current project, schedule and location

What do customers need to prepare in advance?

You don’t have to be completely prepared from the beginning, It's enough to start by telling Fiona the approximate date, city or location, type of shoot, and the feeling you want the photos to leave behind, Other details can be sorted out slowly after consultation
