seven steps

From first message to final delivery

The process will be as simple and clear as possible: first communicate the needs, then confirm the date, location, quotation and reservation fee, and then deliver the film according to the agreed time after shooting

01

Submit inquiry

Start by explaining the type of shoot, date, location, number of people, budget range and the feel you want the image to have, The more specific the information, the more effective the response

02

communication needs

Determine the focus of your wedding, elopement, couples or portrait session and determine if the timeline, location, lighting and scope of services are appropriate

03

Confirm plan

A quote is formed based on the duration of the shoot, deliverables, location and additional requirements, Regular filming areas include Nice and nearby Côte d'Azur locations; Provence, Saint-Tropez or other French cities can be confirmed individually based on dates, transportation and filming schedule, After the shoot is confirmed, 30% of the total price is usually paid as a reservation fee to reserve the date and start pre-production preparations

04

Preparation before shooting

Sort out timelines, outfits, locations, weather options, important relatives and friends, or unmissable scenes to reduce last-minute decisions on the day

05

Formal shooting

Fiona is on hand to observe the actual moment as it happens, providing gesture, position and action guidance when needed

06

Film selection and editing

Fiona does the screening, color grading and foundation processing, By default, the processed high-definition JPG film will be delivered, and the original film will not be delivered

07

Delivery and follow-up

Most individual, couples, travel, proposal or small shoots are usually delivered within 2-4 weeks, More complex projects will have specific lead times confirmed before booking

Consultation can be light

You don’t need to prepare a complete plan from the beginning, First send the date, location, shooting type and the scenes you are interested in, Fiona will first determine the schedule and direction, and then tell you what information needs to be added and whether it is suitable to continue the reservation

Will confirm before booking

The final payment time, cancellation/rescheduling details, additional film price, whether expediting is supported, and whether Provence, Saint-Tropez or other cities in France need to be confirmed separately will all be clearly stated before confirming the shooting
