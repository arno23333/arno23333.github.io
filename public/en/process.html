seven steps

From first message to final delivery

The process stays simple: share your plans, confirm the date, location, quote and booking fee, then receive edited images within the agreed timeline.

01

Send an inquiry

Start with the session type, date, location, number of people, budget range and the feeling you want the images to have. A few clear details help Fiona reply with the right next steps.

02

Talk through your plans

Together we check the focus of your wedding, elopement, couple session or portrait session, including timeline, location, light and service scope.

03

Confirm the details

The quote is based on session length, deliverables, location and additional needs. Nice and nearby French Riviera locations are the regular area; Provence, Saint-Tropez and other French cities can be confirmed separately. Once the session is confirmed, 30% of the total price is usually paid as a booking fee to reserve the date and begin preparation.

04

Plan the session

We prepare the timeline, outfits, locations, weather options, important people and any must-have moments so there are fewer decisions on the day.

05

The session or wedding day

Fiona observes real moments as they happen and gives clear guidance on posture, position and movement when needed.

06

Selection and editing

Fiona selects the images, color grades them and completes light retouching. By default, you receive edited high-resolution JPG files; RAW files are not included.

07

Delivery and follow-up

Most portrait, couple, travel, proposal and small ceremony sessions are delivered within 2-4 weeks. More complex projects have their timeline confirmed before booking.

You can start with a simple message

You do not need a complete plan before writing. Send the date, location, session type and scenes you care about first; Fiona will check availability and tell you what information is still needed.

Everything important is confirmed before booking

Payment timing, cancellation or rescheduling terms, extra edited image pricing, rush delivery options and any travel arrangements for Provence, Saint-Tropez or other French cities are stated before the session is confirmed.

Start consultation

Send your date and idea

You do not need every detail from the start. With a date, location and general session type, Fiona can first check availability and direction.
