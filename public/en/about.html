About

I have lived in France for almost ten years, through study, movement and changing directions, I slowly understood here that photography was what I truly loved

★ Based in Nice, familiar with common shooting locations on the French Riviera ★ You can chat first about personal travel, couples, marriage proposals, and small ceremonies ★ We will review the date, location, light and route together before shooting

I first came to France to study, from undergraduate years through my master’s degree, for a long time, I was learning a new language, a new city and a new way of living, later I realized what truly moved me was using images to keep the light around people and the feeling of a present moment

I love the blue sky, sea breeze, white clouds and Mediterranean sun of southern France, I also love the way people soften during a trip, for me, photography is not about fitting you into a template, it is about photographing you in a real place while keeping your own temperament and relationship intact

In front of the camera, you do not need to prove that you are good at being photographed, my job is to read the light, feel the rhythm and give clear guidance when needed

I try to balance softness and precision, giving the image beautiful light and a southern French atmosphere while still letting the people in the photo look like themselves

If you come back to these photos years later, I hope you remember not only that the day was beautiful, but that you were truly there, free and seen

What I want to keep are those few seconds when you were really close, laughing, and confirming each other

working method

Relaxed, clear and taken care of

Many people come to South France for photos without knowing whether they will feel comfortable on camera, where to go, how to stand or whether the session will feel awkward, Fiona’s role is not to turn you into another person, but to help you relax into the right light and rhythm

Sort out your uncertainties before shooting

You don’t need a complete plan from the start, tell Fiona the date, location, session type and the feeling you prefer, then she will help clarify timing, route, outfits and details to prepare in advance

During the session, guidance and freedom coexist

If you are not used to being photographed, Fiona will give clear and natural guidance on movement and position, if you prefer candid moments, she will also step back to the right distance and preserve the expressions and interactions that naturally happen

After the session, delivery stays clear

After the session, Fiona selects, color grades and lightly retouches the images, then delivers high-resolution JPG files as agreed, you will know when to expect the photos and whether extra edits or other arrangements are needed later

Let’s talk about feelings first

Want to know if it's suitable? Let’s talk first

It is easier to build an accurate plan when you send your date, location, relationship story and favorite visual references, instead of only asking how much
