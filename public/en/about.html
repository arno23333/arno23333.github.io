About

I have lived in France for almost ten years, Reading, moving, and changing my direction, it was also here that I slowly confirmed that photography was what I truly loved

★ Based in Nice, familiar with common shooting locations on the French Riviera ★ You can chat first about personal travel, couples, marriage proposals, and small ceremonies ★ We will review the date, location, light and route together before shooting

I first came to France to study, from undergraduate to master's degree, For a long time, I was learning how to understand a new language, city, and way of life, Later I discovered that what really excites me is to use images to preserve the light of people and the present moment

I like the blue sky, sea breeze, white clouds and sunshine of the Mediterranean in southern France, I also like the way people suddenly relax during the journey, For me, photography is not about posing people into some kind of template, but taking photos in a real place to help you retain your original temperament and relationship

In front of the camera, you don’t need to prove that you’re good at taking photos, My job is to observe the light, judge the rhythm, and give you clear guidance when needed

I try to find a balance between softness and accuracy: to give the picture a beautiful light and a sense of southern French air, but also to make the people in the photo still look like me

If you come back to this set of photos many years from now, I hope you will remember not just that it was a beautiful day, but that you were really there and really felt free and seen

What I want to keep are those few seconds when you were really close, laughing, and confirming each other

working method

Relaxed, clear and taken care of

When many people come to South France to take photos, they are actually not sure whether they are suitable for the camera, where to choose, how to stand, and whether it will be embarrassing that day, Fiona's job is not to turn you into a different person, but to help you slowly relax in the right light and rhythm

Sort out your uncertainties before shooting

You don’t need to have a complete plan ready from the start, Just tell Fiona the date, location, type of shoot and your preferred feel, and together she will figure out the time, route, outfits and details that need to be paid attention to in advance

During shooting, there is guidance and freedom

If you are not used to being photographed, Fiona will give you clear and natural guidance on movements and positions; if you prefer to take snapshots, she will also retreat to a suitable distance, leaving behind the expressions and interactions that really happened

After shooting, delivery remains clear

After the shooting, Fiona will complete the screening, color grading and basic processing, and deliver the high-definition JPG film as agreed, You will know exactly how long it will take to receive the photos, and whether additional photos or other arrangements are needed later

Let’s talk about feelings first

Want to know if it's suitable? Let’s talk first

It's easier to get an accurate plan by sending in your date, location, relationship story, and your favorite visuals than just asking "how much."
