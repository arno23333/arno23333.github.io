Best for

  • You are visiting Nice, Èze, Monaco or nearby towns and want natural portraits.

Usually planned

  • Outfit direction and gentle movement prompts during the session.

Location and light notes

Nice old town, the coastline, hill viewpoints and quieter streets all work well. Early morning or later afternoon usually gives softer light, especially in summer.
