01 / Personal and Travel Personal/Travel Portraits From €299

For solo travelers, personal portraits in South France or a lighter set of images for yourself, the session can feel like an easy walk or become more complete depending on outfit, location and purpose

Best for

  • Traveling alone to Nice, Monaco, Eze or nearby areas
  • Wanting personal portraits that feel natural, not stiff
  • Needing gentle guidance on location, outfit and movement

Usually discussed

  • Date, city and approximate time window
  • Preferred mood: travel, romance, everyday life or personal branding
  • Length of shoot, number of locations and delivery requirements

What to look at when quoting

The final quote usually depends on session length, number of locations, whether makeup or outfit advice is needed, number of final images and any rush delivery request
