01 / Personal and Travel Personal/Travel Portraits From €299

Suitable for those who travel alone, want to leave a set of natural portraits in South France, or need light personal portrait photos, The shoot can be lighthearted or more complete depending on your outfit, location and purpose

Suitable for whom

  • Travel alone to Nice, Monaco, Eze or surrounding areas
  • Want a natural, non-stiff personal portrait
  • Some location, costume and gesture guidance is required

usually communicate

  • Date, city and approximate time period of shooting
  • Desired atmosphere: travel, romance, daily life or image photos
  • Length of shoot, number of locations and delivery requirements

What to look at when quoting

This will usually be confirmed based on the length of the shoot, the number of locations, whether makeup or clothing advice is needed, the number of shots to be delivered and whether there is an urgent need

02 / Couples, Proposals and Engagements Couple/Proposal/Engagement From €399

Ideal for traveling couples, those planning a proposal or an engagement anniversary, Fiona will help you get into the mood through natural interaction, and can also confirm the route and key moments in advance according to the proposal plan

Suitable for whom

  • I want to leave a photo of two people really getting along
  • Planning a proposal, engagement or anniversary
  • I hope the shooting will be a little more relaxed, but still have a sense of ritual in the picture

usually communicate

  • Date, location, and whether the proposal needs to be kept secret
  • What you like about the atmosphere and what you don’t like about the shooting style
  • Length of shoot, number of locations and if route suggestions are needed

What to look at when quoting

It is usually confirmed based on the shooting duration, whether it is a proposal plan, the number of locations, whether advance visits or route suggestions are needed, and the content to be delivered

03 / Elopement and small ceremony Elopement / Small Ceremony From €399

Ideal for a more intimate ceremony with fewer people and a more personally meaningful location, Quotations will take particular account of route, light, traffic, weather alternatives and whether location, route and time suggestions are required from an imaging perspective

Suitable for whom

  • I don’t want to have a big traditional wedding, but I want to retain the sense of ceremony
  • I hope that the picture will be natural, with a sense of environment and story
  • Someone needs to work together to sort out the shooting route

usually communicate

  • Ceremony location, route, light and weather alternatives
  • The duration of the shooting and whether any relatives or friends will participate on the day
  • Do you need confirmation of location, lighting, route and time suggestions from a shooting perspective

What to look at when quoting

This will usually be confirmed based on the ceremony location, route complexity, duration of the shoot, number of participants, weather options, and whether separate transportation or accommodation is required in Provence, Saint-Tropez, or other cities in France

04 / Wedding day Wedding day photography From 1399 EUR

Suitable for newcomers who need to completely record the ceremony, family and friends, venue and live rhythm, The quote will not only look at the number of shooting hours, but also the location, number of people, complexity of the process and scope of delivery

Suitable for whom

  • With formal ceremony or full wedding day arrangements
  • I hope someone can communicate the timeline and key scenes in advance
  • It needs to be delivered steadily, not just a few blockbusters

usually communicate

  • Ceremony time, venue, number of people and procedures of the day
  • Whether you need to shoot prep, ceremony, photo session, dinner or party
  • Number of sheets to be delivered, delivery period and whether overtime is required

What to look at when quoting

Wedding day photography starts from €1399 and is usually confirmed based on the wedding process, hours of filming, number of people, number of venues, whether dinner or party recording is required, scope of delivery, and transport arrangements in Provence, Saint-Tropez or other cities in France
